# Fact Table: FactSales

## Purpose

The `FactSales` table is the central fact table of the Sales Analytics Data Warehouse.

It stores sales transactions at the order-item level and provides the quantitative measures required for business intelligence, reporting, and analytics.

---

# Business Process

Sales

---

# Grain

**One record represents one product sold within one customer order (one order item).**

---

# Primary Key

| Column | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| SalesKey | Surrogate key generated by the Data Warehouse |

---

# Degenerate Dimensions

- OrderID
- OrderItemID

---

# Foreign Keys

- DateKey
- CustomerKey
- ProductKey
- SellerKey
- PaymentKey

---

# Measures

- SalesAmount
- FreightAmount
- PaymentValue
- PaymentInstallments
- ReviewScore

---

# Source Tables

- Orders
- OrderItems
- Payments
- Reviews

---

# Business Usage

Supports reporting such as:

- Revenue by Product
- Revenue by Category
- Revenue by Seller
- Revenue by Customer
- Revenue by State
- Revenue by Payment Method
- Sales Trend Analysis
- Customer Satisfaction Analysis
