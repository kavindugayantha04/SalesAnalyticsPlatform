# Dimension: DimDate

## Purpose

Stores calendar information for all analytical reporting.

This dimension allows users to analyze sales by:

- Day
- Month
- Quarter
- Year
- Weekday
- Weekend

without recalculating date values in Power BI.

---

## Source

Generated by SQL during warehouse creation.

---

## Primary Key

DateKey

Format:

YYYYMMDD

Example:

20180115

---

## Attributes

- DateKey
- FullDate
- Day
- Month
- MonthName
- Quarter
- Year
- Weekday
- WeekdayNumber
- IsWeekend

---

## Example

| DateKey | FullDate | Month | Year |
|----------|-----------|-------|------|
|20180101|2018-01-01|January|2018|
